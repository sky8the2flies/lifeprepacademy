College, Career & Life Planning

Empowering Our Future Workforce

The LifePrep Academy™ College and Career Planning curriculum helps prepare youth for life after high school.


We teach students and their parents the college knowledge, attitudes and skills students need to graduate from high school college-ready. Workshop participants learn academic planning strategies, college and career options, how to access internet-based resources, the types of financial aid available to them and more.



Engaging Parents and Families

An effective solution to California's more than 600:1 student-to-counselor ratio!


Through LifePrep Academy workshops and webinars, parents:

• Learn how to help their child develop an academic plan for their college and career goals;

• Gain confidence in their understanding on how they can help their child prepare for life after high school; and

• Are equipped for clear, high quality communication and collaboration with school counselors.

            

    LifePrep Academy Covers:


  • A-G Requirements, AP & Honors, Concurrent Classes

  • Financial Aid, Scholarships, Grants and Loans

  • UC, CSU, Community Colleges and Private Universities

  • Studying Abroad, Apprenticeships, School-to-Career

  • Career Exploration, Life Skills and much more.

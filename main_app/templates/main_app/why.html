Why LifePrep Academy?

By empowering families to advocate for their childâ€™s transition to life after high school, we can increase the number of low-to-middle income and first generation youth that graduate from high school college ready and prepared to enter the workforce.

With student-to-counselor ratios of more than 600:1 in many California schools, parent engagement is critical to ensuring youth reach their potential. LifePrep Academy resources and webinars teach families how to efficiently work with counselors to achieve the best results for youth.

Since 1998, hundreds of schools, nonprofits, churches and colleges have relied upon the LifePrep Academy to prepare youth for life after high school.

We offer schools and districts a cost effective alternative to Naviance, Xello and other online programs for achieving their LCAP goals. Professional development trainings are available for counselors, teachers and administrators - along with workshops or webinars for students and parents.

Site license models are available that provide complete access to LifePrep Academy courses to every enrolled student and their parents. LifePrep Academy is the perfect resource for schools and districts that provide iPads, Chromebooks or other devices for their students.

Schools and districts receive a co-branded dashboard for their families, where students and parents can access the LifePrep Academy curriculum, California-based scholarships, a database of homework help sites, a list of their favorite sites, pre- and post-activity surveys and more.

Through Donors Choose, schools can provide the LifePrep Academy digital curriculum and webinars to students and families AT NO COST.

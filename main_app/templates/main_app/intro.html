Today is the First Day of Your Future!

You are about to begin a very exciting and challenging phase of your life. Having a plan to graduate is the start of your plan to succeed. You also know that your family is an important part of helping you plan for your time in high school and for those years after high school.

Today you are embarking on a journey that will provide the stepping stones to a great life. Your first step is getting ready to go, passport in hand—high school diploma—on your journey.

The LifePrep Academy has been designed to assist you and your family in planning for high school graduation and success in life. The curriculum can help you chart the itinerary for your trip; it is divided into eight major sections:

SECTION I: Middle School: Ready to Grow

SECTION II: Getting Prepared: Your High School Diploma

SECTION III: The Journey: Life After High School

SECTION IV: Health Living and Effective Personal Skills

SECTION V: Homework Help Sites

SECTION VI: Scholarship Search Engines

SECTION VII: Notes

SECTION VIII: Favorite Links

Section I focuses on developing the right mind frame in the middle school years to prepare for high school. Section II discusses resources you'll need to get your high school diploma, your real passport to the future. Section III starts with life after high school and explores the many post secondary options you have before you. Section IV covers the role of diet, exercise and sleep in helping students achieve success during the high school years and beyond.

Section V provides a database of more than 2,500 educational websites that students can use to do research, find help with their homework and further their studies. Section VI offers a database of hundreds of California-based scholarships and search engines. Section VII allow students to take notes on the important topics they learn while using the LifePrep Academy, and Section VIII is a list of the students favorite LifePrep web links.
